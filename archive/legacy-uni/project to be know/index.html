⚡ Simple Process

How UniConnect works

Six steps — from creating your account to redeeming real discounts at campus venues.

📧
01
Register with university email
Sign up using your official Philadelphia University email. Only enrolled students can join — verified against the university registry. You receive +50 welcome points instantly.
🙋
02
Ask questions or help others
Post academic questions (costs 10 pts) and answer others to earn points back. Better answers get more upvotes. Best Answer earns +15 pts bonus automatically.
🪙
03
Earn points for every contribution
Every helpful action earns points — upvotes, best answers, private tutoring sessions, daily logins. Reach 1,000 pts to unlock Verified Tutor badge.
💬
04
Request private tutoring sessions
Need deeper help? Request a one-on-one private chat session with a verified tutor. Rate your session to help others in the future.
🏆
05
Climb the faculty leaderboard
Monthly rankings for each faculty. Top contributors earn special recognition and unlock exclusive rewards. Compete with classmates in a friendly academic environment.
🎁
06
Redeem at campus venues
Exchange points for real discounts at the university cafeteria, library, bookshop, print centre and more. A QR code is sent to your university email — scan it at the venue.
✨ Platform Features

Built for university life

Every feature designed around how students actually study, ask for help, and collaborate.

🔍
Smart Question Feed
Browse questions by faculty, course, or topic. Live search finds existing answers before you post — saving your 10 pts asking cost.
✅
Best Answer System
Question owners mark the most helpful answer. Best answers are pinned at the top and earn the contributor an automatic +15 pt reward.
💬
Private Tutoring Chat
One-on-one real-time chat with verified tutors. Sessions are private and rated by both parties for transparency and quality.
🪙
Points & Campus Rewards
Earn points helping others and spend them at real campus locations — cafeteria, library, bookshop, print centre, gym and more.
🏆
Faculty Leaderboard
Monthly rankings per faculty. Top 3 contributors earn special recognition badges and exclusive rewards for their academic community.
🛡️
AI-Assisted Moderation
Content reviewed by AI and human admins. Report inappropriate posts anytime — admins review within 24 hours to keep the platform safe.
